Run Flow

  1. Pregame countdown gives players time to place units.
  2. Combat starts the wave and resolves until one side is wiped.
  3. Wave clear grants session money and opens intermission voting.
  4. Defeat ends the run and grants profile money scaled by wave and difficulty.

Co-op

Enemy Scaling

Co-op increases spawn count with a sublinear multiplier: 1 + 0.5 x (players - 1). Enemy health, damage, and attack speed match solo at the same wave and difficulty.
